LOVD

Land Vehicle Detection in Complex Scenes of Optical Remote Sensing Image.


Introduction to LOVD



LOVD is an abbreviation for 'Land Vehicle Detection in Complex Scenes of Optical Remote Sensing Image'. It is built with over 1000 remote sensing images and tested with state-of-art algorithms.


Dataset Description


LOVD is a large dataset for vehicle detection on remote sensing images. It focuses on the vehicle detection and establishes one of the largest remote sensing dataset. The images are all collected by our team with UAV equipment. LOVD contains 1196 pictures and 13 types of vehicles. Each picture is in size of 5280*3956 pixels and taken in various background like river, city, green land. LOVD not only considers different background objects, but also takes weather scene into consideration. It contains pictures taken on fog weather.

All of those instances are annotated with rotated bounding box and have a excellent balance in orientation, density and shape.
