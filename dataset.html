LOVD

Land Vehicle Detection in Complex Scenes of Optical Remote Sensing Image.


Data source and copyright



All the LOVD images are collected by our teams with UAV equipment. They are all in jpg format and RGB images All images with annotations files can only be used for academic purpose and cannot be applied for any commercial use.

Classification and annotation characteristic



LOVD contains 13 types of vehicles: cars, trucks, dump trucks, mixer trucks, trailers, bulldozers, pickup trucks, oil tankers, excavators, buses, school buses, trucks and cranes. Our annotation files are in xml format. The format is demonstrated below:



As shown in the picture, the filename of xml file corresponds with pictures. The 'size' tag shows the size of related pictures and each 'object' tag corresponds to annotation information of a vehicle target. The three-level tag under 'object' is essential information. 'type' means the whether the object is annotated with rotated bounding box. 'name' corresponds to the type name of the vehicle target and both Chinese and English name are given in the xml. If you do not need Chinese tag, you can transfer it. 'difficult' means whether it is difficult to annotate it by people. 'robndbox' contains the key information of targets' location and orientation. We try to include the information of vehicle direction in our dataset, so we add the direction of vehicle's head by applying a 'red_point' method. The 'red_point' coordinate is the corresponding point on the front left of the car. Each point is in format(x,y), and we annotate the vehicle in a clockwise order. 'angle' is just a supplementary information whose range is in (0,2*pi). You can also calculate the angle by four coordinates. We take the horizontal to the right as the initial benchmark, and calculate the angle counterclockwise.

Image Info

The image contains the pixel resolution, timestamp, camera information and GPS info. For privacy safety, we only keep resolution info in the public version. If you need more info, please contact us.
